# Steps

1 go to the cloudfront create a distribution name it as per your preference

2 on custom domain option enter your domain name. then click next

3 specify origin type Amazon s3 and in Origin section select you s3 bucket.

4 now after selecting bucket you will see and alert option underneath the s3 bucket, click on use website endpoint

5 leave everything default, and click next.

6 web application firewall WAF you can enable it if you want to. I prefer disable cause it is paid.

7 TLS certificate show error if you didn't define certificate in us-east-1 region earlier

8 choose your certificate then click on next.

9 review all the option the next and now your cloud front is created
